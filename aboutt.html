About Us

Our Objective

We have a three-fold Objective:

  • Develop sports ecosystems by building a broad base of recreational players, selecting the best to compete at higher levels.
  • Incorporate global training methodologies in India by bringing in international coaches and upskilling local teams.
  • Foster a community of basketball players with easy access to expert coaching, leagues, tournaments, and camps, providing opportunities to passionate young athletes.

Our Safety Regulations

  • All our programs are conducted in secure gated premises with a security guard present to maintain a visitor register.
  • Coaches remain on-site until each child is picked up by a parent or guardian.
  • All our coaches are trained in first aid, and medical kits are available at every center.
  • Emergency contact numbers for each child are held by their respective academy coach.
  • Regular background checks are conducted on all team members.
  • Medical conditions for each child are documented, and precautions are taken as needed.

Founder of Pitch Basketball Academy

Chidanand Kumar

An expert in building community sports programs, Chidanand Kumar is the founder of Pitch Sports, which operates its sports academies across the country. Pitch Sports has been running coaching programs with a qualified team of coaches since 2011. Working with schools, residential societies, clubs, and sports goods stores, Pitch Sports currently has over 1,000 children enrolled in more than 25 locations across Bangalore, Chennai, and Kochi.
