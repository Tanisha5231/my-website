Welcome to Pitch Basketball Coaching Academy

Pitch Sports is the largest network of basketball players and academies in India. We are the leaders in transforming the grassroots basketball landscape since 2011. With a growing footprint in India, Pitch Sports currently conducts basketball coaching programmes for over 1000 children in 20 locations across Bangalore, Delhi and Hyderabad. We specialize in international-style basketball camps and academies. Comprising a team of certified and qualified coaches, Pitch Sports works with some of the most prestigious schools and institutions. Several children from Pitch Sports academies are selected to state and national teams every year.
